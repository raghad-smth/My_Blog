Hey there ♡, I'm Raghad, a Computer Science student, and I’m excited to welcome you to my blog! If you’re reading this, it means I’ve taken a significant step toward realizing my dream of sharing my thoughts and experiences with the world.

From a young age, I’ve developed my own philosophy and formed unique opinions about life, relationships, and everything in between. Growing up as the eldest sibling granted me plenty of time to reflect before my younger siblings arrived. This solitude, combined with my expansive imagination, led to a treasure trove of thoughts. Initially, I wrote them down for fun, but it quickly became clear how fulfilling it was to express myself. That’s when I thought, “Why not share this?”

Through this blog, I aim to connect with others who share similar thoughts and perhaps help someone navigate a challenging time by resonating with my writing. This space is for the younger version of me who yearned to share her thoughts with the world, for those who have shaped who I am today, and for anyone I might inspire or open their eyes to a new perspective.

My Believes

  • Start where you are, work with what you have, and do what you can.
  • Everything happens for a reason, and it happens for you, never against you.
  • No one is obligated to lift you up; it’s your responsibility to take care of yourself.
  • You will find what you seek. If you look for envy, anger, and hate, you’ll find them. If you seek forgiveness, love, and peace, they will be yours.
  • The day you stop growing is the day you start dying. Never let a day go by without growth.
  • Become obsessed with yourself; it’s a project that will never let you down.
